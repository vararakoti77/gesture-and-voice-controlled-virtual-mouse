# AI Virtual Assistant - Voice Commands Guide

Welcome to the voice command documentation for your OpenCV AI Virtual Mouse! Here is the complete list of everything you can control hands-free.

---

### 🖱️ 1. Core Mouse Movement & Clicks
- **"left click"** or **"click"**: Performs a standard left mouse click.
- **"right click"**: Performs a right mouse click.
- **"double click"**: Performs a fast double left-click (useful for opening apps or files on screen!).
- **"scroll up"**: Scrolls up the page.
- **"scroll down"**: Scrolls down the page.
- **"move up"** or **"up"**: Jumps the cursor up 40 pixels.
- **"move down"** or **"down"**: Jumps the cursor down 40 pixels.
- **"move left"** or **"left"**: Jumps the cursor left 40 pixels.
- **"move right"** or **"right"**: Jumps the cursor right 40 pixels.

### 🎥 2. Media & Screen Controls
- **"play"** or **"pause"**: Plays or pauses currently running media (Spotify, YouTube, etc).
- **"volume up"**: Increases system volume by 5%.
- **"volume down"**: Decreases system volume by 5%.
- **"brightness up"**: Increases screen brightness by 10%.
- **"brightness down"**: Decreases screen brightness by 10%.
- **"screenshot"** or **"screen shot"**: Takes a screenshot and saves it to your screenshots folder.
- **"zoom in"**: Triggers `Ctrl + =` to zoom into web pages or documents.
- **"zoom out"**: Triggers `Ctrl + -` to zoom out.

### 🪟 3. Window & Tab Management
- **"desktop"** or **"minimize all"**: Minimizes all windows and shows your desktop (`Win + D`).
- **"mini"** or **"minimize"**: Minimizes the current active window (`Win + Down`).
- **"maxi"** or **"maximize"**: Maximizes the current active window (`Win + Up`).
- **"switch window"**, **"switch app"**, or **"alt tab"**: Switches to your last active application (`Alt + Tab`).
- **"new window"** or **"open new window"**: Opens a new application window (`Ctrl + N`).
- **"new tab"** or **"open new tab"**: Opens a new tab in the active browser or app (`Ctrl + T`).
- **"next window"** or **"go to next window"**: Switches forward to the next open application (`Alt + Tab`).
- **"previous tab"** or **"go to previous tab"**: Switches you to the tab natively on the left (`Ctrl + Shift + Tab`).

### 🚀 4. OS & Application Launching
- **"open [app name]"**: Will attempt to open ANY app installed on your system or run a web search if it doesn't exist.
  - *Example:* "open calculator", "open spotify", "open paint", "open discord".
- **"close"**, **"close window"**, or **"close [app name]"**: Immediately closes your current active application (`Alt + F4`).
  - *Example:* "close chrome", "close notepad", "close discord".
- **"open chrome"** or **"open browser"**: Opens Google Chrome.
- **"open notepad"**: Opens Notepad.
- **"open calculator"**: Opens the Calculator.
- **"open settings"**: Opens Windows Settings.
- **"open file"** or **"open explorer"**: Opens Windows File Explorer.
- **"open vs code"** or **"open visual studio code"**: Opens Visual Studio Code.
- **"open word"** or **"open microsoft word"**: Opens Microsoft Word.
- **"open whatsapp"**: Directly launches the WhatsApp desktop application.
- **"open youtube"**: Automatically launches your browser directly to YouTube.com.

### 📂 5. Deep File & Search Lookups
- **"open file [file name]"**: Rapidly searches your computer (starting with Documents, Desktop, Downloads, and Pictures) to locate and natively open the exact file you asked for.
- **"open folder [folder name]"**: Rapidly searches your computer to locate and natively open the exact folder you asked for in File Explorer.
- **"open whatsapp chat [Contact Name]"**: Opens WhatsApp, searches for the name, and enters their chat hands-free.

### ⌨️ 6. Typing, Searching & Text Editing
- **"type [anything]"**: Instantly types exactly what you said. *(Example: "type hello world")*
- **"search [anything]"** or **"search for [anything]"**: Opens your browser and instantly searches for it on Google.
- **"open search bar"**, **"find"**, or **"search this page"**: Drops down the search/find bar natively inside ANY app (Chrome, Explorer, WhatsApp, VS Code) using `Ctrl + F`.
- **"select all"** or **"select everything"**: Instantly highlights all text natively (`Ctrl + A`).
- **"save"** or **"save file"**: Saves your active document natively (`Ctrl + S`).
- **"copy"** or **"copy text"**: Copies highlighted text natively (`Ctrl + C`).
- **"paste"** or **"paste text"**: Pastes text natively (`Ctrl + V`).
- **"cut"** or **"cut text"**: Cuts highlighted text natively (`Ctrl + X`).
- **"undo"** or **"undo that"**: Undoes your last action natively (`Ctrl + Z`).
- **"redo"** or **"redo that"**: Redoes your last action natively (`Ctrl + Y`).
- **"refresh"**, **"reload"**, or **"refresh page"**: Refreshes the current page or window (`F5`).
- **"enter"**, **"hit enter"**, or **"press enter"**: Hits the Enter key natively.
- **"backspace"**, **"clear"**, or **"clear that"**: Hits the Backspace key natively.
- **"delete"** or **"erase"**: Hits the Delete key natively.
- **"space"**, **"spacebar"**, or **"hit space"**: Hits the Spacebar natively.

### 🔴 7. Critical Power States
- **"shutdown computer"** or **"shut down computer"**: Starts a 5-second shutdown sequence.
- **"restart computer"**: Starts a 5-second restart sequence.
- **"quit"**, **"terminate"**, or **"exit"**: Safely unhooks the microphone and closes the AI Virtual Mouse program itself.
